A little bit about myself...

I'm a professional programmer in scripting languages (bash, TCL, etc). Due to various circumstances, I decided to change the direction of my activity and try myself in web development.

Previous skills/achivements:

The development, design and implementation of automatic generation system for the fork of UNIX-like OS – build, prepare and generate an installation media from scratch. The design and implementation of the GUI-installer for the operation system. The design and implementation GUI wrapper for network, daemons or services management and removable media subsystem.

Much earlier has been worked as the system administrator. Wrote scripts on Visual Basic for AutoCAD: automated calculation of structures of mobile advertising and information stands with the issuance of drawings and item-by-item specifications; determination of discontinuities (integrity) of the contours of geometric shapes before sending to the CNC; cleaning the G-code of control programs for laser cutting / engraving machines from "parasitic" objects.

I also have sufficient experience in writing thematic texts (ukraininan/russian) for the website content (copywriting). For several years I was engaged in 3D-modeling and vector graphics. I can do pre-processing (retouching) images, as well as their optimization to reduce server load and improve the overall performance of the website.
